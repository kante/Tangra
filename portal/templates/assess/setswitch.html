Set Switching

In this task you will be making categorical decisions about digits and letters.

Before we begin, please place your index fingers on the Z and M keys of the keyboard.

When you are ready, press SPACE to proceed to the instructions for the first block of trials.

Digits Block

In this block, you will be making decisions about digits.

First, a + will appear in the centre of the screen. Keep your eyes on the +.
After a second, the + will be replaced by a digit.

Digits will be even (2,4,6,8) or odd (1,3,5,7).

If the digit is even, please press Z as quickly as possible.
If the digit is odd, please press M as quickly as possible.

When you are ready, press SPACE to start the block.

Digits/Letters Block

If the digit is even, please press Z as quickly as possible.
