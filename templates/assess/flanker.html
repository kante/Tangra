Flankers Task

In this task you will see a 5x5 table of symbols. The central symbol will be an arrow pointing left or right.

All other symbols will be the same, and they will either be arrows pointing left, arrows pointing right or squares.

Your task is to determine whether the central symbol is facing left or right.

Before we begin, please place your index fingers on the Z and M keys of the keyboard.

If the arrow in the middle is facing left, please press Z.
If the arrow in the middle is facing right, please press M.

Please try to respond as quickly and accurately as possible.

When you are ready, press SPACE to proceed to the instructions for the first block of trials.

Break
