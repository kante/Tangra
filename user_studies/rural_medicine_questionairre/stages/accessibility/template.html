Accessibility of Health Care Services

Background Information

It is estimated that 20-30% of Canadians live in a rural setting while many health care facilities exist in urban centers. This leaves rural residents an average 60 km commute to family physicians. Adequate access to health care services is vital to health maintenance and initiatives to increase rural physician recruitment in conjunction with programs to increase rural community access to health care providers are needed to assure no disparities in health care in rural settings.

Goals

  • Increased patient roster promotion
  • Programs for physician recruitment and retention
  • Programs to facilitate telemedicine and timely referral to higher levels of care.
